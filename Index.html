About me

Hello, my name is Ethan Jones, I am a current student at the amazing Coder Foundry School. I am learning the fundamentals about code and web design at a fast but efficient way. I chose to attend Coder Foundry for one simple reason, I love computers. I always have and will. It started as purely a hobby of mine at a young age, but turned into someone I love then most. I would open and take apart my old computers to see how they worked, only to then try and put them back together. So, my interests being as they are, I wanted to expand my learning and knowledge. So, I became a student at Coder Foundry for that singular purpose. To learn code! My dreams about my future career as a coder are vast indeed, so like with any and everything that I have an interest in learning, I take it serious. I plan to make an outstanding career, with the hard work I do to prove it.

I see myself learning a vast amount of coding practices. I want to be able to pick up a project and accurately complete it with ease. I see myself being able to help others with their project and work, as well as introducing others to the world of coding. Yes, it may seem impossible at first, especially for a beginner such as myself, but through hard work and repetition, you will breath in a very healthy amount of knowledge and know how.

All in all, I plan to lead a successful and engaging coding career and lifestyle. As well as helping other coding enthusiasts along their way to success.
